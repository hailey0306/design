Overview

PROBLEM STATEMENT

Interracial relationship is on the rise in the US. However, language can prove to be a communication barrier if interracial people do not adequately understand each others' language. It is frustrating and maddening to sit in front of another human being (One that you wish to befriend because god knows you need one in this new country.) and have ideas, opinions and stories build up like hot steam in a pressure cooker but with no way of expressing them, and transitively, no way of expressing yourself.

THE GOAL

Use technology to improve the dating experience of people who is in an interracial relationship.

DURATION

5 Weeks

MY ROLE

Branding, Concepting, Researcher, Animater.
